* Part III — Ring isomorphism theorems
** DONE Second isomorphism theorem for rings
   For subring A and ideal B:
   - A + B is a subring
   - A ∩ B is an ideal of A
   - (A + B)/B ≅ A/(A ∩ B)

** DONE Third isomorphism theorem for rings
   For ideals I ⊆ J:
   - J/I is an ideal of R/I
   - (R/I)/(J/I) ≅ R/J

** DONE Fourth / lattice isomorphism theorem for rings
   For ideal I:
   - subrings containing I correspond to subrings of R/I
   - ideals containing I correspond to ideals of R/I
