* Goal
  Formalize:
  - the canonical/classifying category Cl(Th) of an algebraic theory Th
  - the generic model G in Cl(Th)
  - the equivalence between finite-product-preserving functors out of Cl(Th)
    and models of Th

  - the equivalence Cl(Th(C)) ~ C
  - translations between algebraic theories via classifying categories
  - soundness/completeness via the generic model

  syntax <-> category with finite products
